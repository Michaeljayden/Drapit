**Drapit adds AI-powered virtual try-on to your Shopify store in minutes — no developer needed.**

Shoppers upload a photo and instantly see how any garment looks on their body. The result appears directly on your product page, with a buy button right next to it. Fewer returns. Higher conversion. More confident shoppers.

---

### How it works

1. **Install the app** — Drapit automatically adds a "Try it on" button to your product pages.
2. **Shopper clicks the button** — A lightweight widget opens. No new page, no redirect.
3. **Upload & process** — The shopper uploads a photo. Our AI fits the garment to their body in seconds.
4. **See the result** — A realistic try-on image appears. The shopper can buy directly from there.

That's it. The entire experience happens on your product page without disrupting your store's design.

---

### Why merchants choose Drapit

**Reduce returns**
Returns cost fashion stores 20–30% of revenue. Virtual try-on gives shoppers confidence before they buy — leading to fewer "it didn't look like I expected" returns.

**Increase conversion**
Shoppers who interact with virtual try-on convert at significantly higher rates. They've already mentally committed to the purchase before clicking "Add to cart."

**Works with any theme**
Drapit works with all Shopify themes — free and paid. Dawn, Debut, Impulse, custom themes — it doesn't matter. The widget integrates without touching your existing design.

**Privacy-first by design**
Shopper photos are processed and immediately discarded. We never store personal images. GDPR-compliant out of the box.

**Fully customizable widget**
Match the widget to your brand: change the button color, text, and style from your dashboard. No code required.

**Real-time analytics**
Track how many try-ons happen per product, identify your best-performing items, and see how virtual try-on impacts your conversion rate — all from one dashboard.

### Frequently asked questions

**Does Drapit work with all Shopify themes?**
Yes. Drapit works with every Shopify theme — free or paid. The app automatically configures the necessary script tags in your theme.

**Do I need a developer to install it?**
No. Installation is fully automatic through the Shopify app flow. No code changes needed.

**Are shopper photos stored?**
No. Photos are processed by our AI and immediately deleted. We never store or share shopper images.

**Can I control which products show the try-on button?**
Yes. You can enable or disable the try-on button per product from your dashboard.

**What counts as a "try-on"?**
Only completed AI generations count — when a shopper uploads a photo and receives a result. Button clicks alone don't count.

**What if I need help?**
Email us at info@drapit.io. We typically respond within one business day.
